A) General

1) What is this document?

This document is a FAQ related to common questions and issues about Newton Message Pad hardware, software and sources.

2) Where do I find the latest version?

This FAQ will be posted as a plain text document monthly on comp.sys.newton.misc, news.answers and comp.answers.

The archive is [will be?] hosted on:

  • PlanetNewton: http://www.planetnewton.com/ [Not yet]
  • ThisOldNewt: http://members.aol.com/thisolnewt/faq/
  • Chris Stephens' site: http://www.cat2.com/newton/newton-faq.htm
  • Info-Newt: http://www.info-newt.com/faq/
